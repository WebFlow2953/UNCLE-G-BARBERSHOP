Frequently Asked Questions

The duration depends on the size and complexity of the project. A standard residential ceiling installation typically takes 2-3 days, while larger commercial projects may take 1-2 weeks. During our free consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline for your specific project.

Gypsum ceilings offer a smooth, seamless finish ideal for living areas and bedrooms, with excellent fire resistance. PVC ceilings are moisture-resistant, making them perfect for kitchens and bathrooms. Gypsum provides a more premium look, while PVC is more practical for wet areas.
